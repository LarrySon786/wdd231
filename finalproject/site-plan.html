Site Name is 'Travel Made Easy'

Reason: The purpose of this website is to allow users to sign up for vacations. They will have easy access to all the information that they need.

Scenarios

1 Where would be a good place to go on vacation?

2 What can I do in _____ (vacation places name)

3 What Hotels Could we stay at?

4 When Could I leave?

5 How much would it cost?

Color Schemes:

- #00458b deep blue

- #99ddff sky blue

- #3fd2c7 light teal

Color Scheme was selected to be kind of like the skys colors (since they fly on vacation)
